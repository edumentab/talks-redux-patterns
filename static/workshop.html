Redux Patterns

Are you among those who find that Redux a bit scary? Or you've made your peace with it, but find it a bit verbose and boilerplaty? Maybe you just want to be a bit inspired regarding how to use it and what's possible? If the answer to any of these questions is yes, then this workshop is for you!

In the workshop we will explore a small example React application that consumes a backend API and uses Redux for state management. At the outset the setup is rather orthodox, which gives you a chance to get up to speed with a normal Redux setup.

Then, as the workshop progresses, you will be taken through a series of increasingly unorthodox refactors. Our hope is that this will both provide you with new ideas, and make you more comfortable with Redux in general!

While Redux is the focus, we'll also get the opportunity to talk about TypeScript concepts (to the extent that the audience so wishes). There will also be a fair bit of discussion around unit testing.

The workshop can be a simple 1-2 hour lecture, or be extended to include code-along. If you have a Redux codebase ripe for improving and/or experimenting, it could also be rewarding to end the workshop with a refactoring session where we help you employ the discussed patterns.

If this sounds interesting then you can peek at the material here, and contact us at info@edument.se!
